# Viscera Flat

A game jam entry (Godot 4). You grow and graft mutations onto a living house — balancing Light and Moisture stats as the house-tree evolves — until it blooms.

## Systems

- Data-driven house parts and mutations (`data/house_parts.json`, `data/mutations.json`, `data/mutation_meta_impact.json`)
- Drag-to-graft mutation cards onto the house, with live stat/tooltip feedback
- Growth animation, window mood indicators (color/scale driven by Light & Moisture)
- End-game bloom sequence

## Status

Jam scope, not actively maintained.
